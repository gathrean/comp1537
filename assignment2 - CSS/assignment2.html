Best Fried Rice Recipe (The Best)

This life-changing recipe will change your life!

Ingredients:

  • Butter: This is game changer! This is the same stuff they use at hibachi restaurants for their fried rice. It adds a lot more flavor and helps to brown the rice (Fun Fact: Butter burns fast!). You can always use other cooiking oil but butter is the best if you want the best.
  • Eggs: This is for the gym bros out there. Eggs are eggcellent if you want to add extra protein for your fried rice. You can also subtitute tofu if you are allergic to eggs or just not a big egg fan.
  • Vegetables: You can use diced carrots, onions, garlic, and peas. Feel free to use whatever veggies you have on hand. Heck, you can use bell peppers, zucchini, leeks, corn, and edamame. That is all good.
  • Rice: Of course! What is a fried rice recipe without rice! You can use any type of rice that you prefer. If you want a rice that cooks up fluffy and does not clump togther, use long grain white rice or jasmine rice.
  • Soy sauce: I always use low sodium soy sauce however dark or regular soy sauce can be substituted just make sure to dilute it a bit with water to cut down on the saltiness.
  • Oyster Sauce Is a thick savory slightly sweet and salty sauce that can be found in the international or asian section of your grocery store.
  • For serving: I like to top my stir fried rice with green onions and sesame seeds for garnish.
  • Sesame oil: Adds a wonderfully nutty flavor to this recipe. A little bit goes a long way!

How to make the Best Fried Rice

  1. Start by heating 1 tablespoon of butter into a large skillet. Add the eggs and scramble until fully cooked.
  2. Remove them from the pan and set aside. Add the remaining tablespoon of butter into the pan. Add carrots and chopped onion to the pan and cook until tender, 3-4 minutes. Stir in garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Add in the cold rice and peasand saut√© for 3-4 minutes. The rice should brown up a bit.
  4. Add the eggs back to the pan and stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce and sesame oil. Cook for 1-2 minutes to heat through.
  5. Serve immediately with green onionsfor garnish, if desired.
  6. Enjoy!
